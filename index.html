Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

Colorfall (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, your rights under applicable laws, and how you can contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Personal Data You Provide: Account details such as username and password if you create an account.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Device information, operating system, advertising ID, usage data, crash logs, approximate location.
  • Third-Party Data:
    • Google AdMob: collects device info, advertising ID, usage data, approximate location, for ads.
    • Unity Analytics: collects device and session info, events, crash data, for analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data may be used to provide app features, display ads (via AdMob), analyze usage (via Unity Analytics), improve performance, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

We rely on consent, contract, legitimate interest, and legal obligations as the legal bases for processing personal data.

4. Your Rights

GDPR (EU/UK): Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, withdrawal of consent.

CCPA/CPRA (California, USA): Disclosure, deletion, opt-out from sale/sharing (we do not sell), non-discrimination.

You can also opt out of personalized ads in device settings (e.g., Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out).

5. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We apply technical and organizational safeguards against unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Data Retention

Data is kept only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, then securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or local legal age). If such data is found, we delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence with safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
